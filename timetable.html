The Non-Negotiable Rules
🧘
15 Min Meditation — Every Day
No exceptions. Not even exam days. Not even days you "don't feel like it". This is the cognitive foundation the rest of the schedule rests on.
🏋️
Never Cut Gym to Study
Physical training improves cognitive performance and retention. There is no scenario where skipping gym gives you more study benefit than the cognitive cost to your brain.
😴
10 PM Hard Stop — Sleep
7-8 hours sleep. Non-negotiable. Sleep deprivation reduces memory consolidation by up to 40%. You cannot study until 1 AM and also learn effectively. Pick one.
📵
Phone Off During Deep Work
Not on silent — OFF. Or in another room. A single notification kills 23 minutes of focus on average. Your 3-hour block becomes 1 effective hour without this rule.
📝
3 Specific Tasks Every Sunday
Write exact Monday–Wednesday tasks: file names, function names, specific problems. "Study LangChain" → zero. "Build retrieve() function in journal_rag.py" → done.
⚡
Never Zero Application Days
3 applications minimum, every day including exams. Your pipeline is your safety net. Stopping applications for 3 weeks kills momentum that takes weeks to restart.
